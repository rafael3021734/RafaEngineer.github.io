How I work

My Process

01

Phase 1

Discovery Call

I will delve deep into your world to fully comprehend who you are, what your problems are, and who your target market is. This is crucial for creating a landing page that performs well and serves your goals correctly.

02

Phase 2

Designing your landing page

Using what I have discovered about you, I will develop a landing page that fulfils your specific requirements, accurately represents your brand, and is both aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly for your target audience.

03

Phase 3

Building your landing page

Once you provide your approval, I will start building the landing page while ensuring that I adhere as closely as possible to current web standards. I want to meet your needs with a high-performing, secure, and dependable solution.

04

Phase 4

Launching your landing page

After the build is finished and you have approved of the design, the time has come to launch your landing page. I will provide you with training during this phase so you can update your it with new content on your own.

Who is Annie

About Me

I am Annie and I design and devlop landing pages. I currently live in London, with my daughter and 2 cats!

Before I entered the world of tech I did a variety of roles, some of which include running my own business and gaining a degree in Politics and Development Studies.

When the covid pandemic hit, I had to change careers and I started to learn to code and design. I found that I enjoyed crafting high-converting, beautiful and user-friendly landing pages.

It was a challenging time but my motto is: If there is something I don’t know, I learn it. If I have a problem, I'll figure out a solution!
